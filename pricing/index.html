Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Who I Met for free?
Absolutely! Our Free plan gives you up to 50 contacts and 6 months of meeting history at no cost, forever. No credit card required. Just connect your Google Calendar and you're ready to go.
What happens when I hit the contact limit?
You'll still have full access to your existing contacts and all features. You just won't be able to add new contacts until you upgrade to a higher plan or remove some existing contacts. We'll never delete your data.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your paid plan features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will revert to the Free plan.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days of your subscription start date and we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.
