Everything You Need to Manage Your Network

Who I Met turns your calendar into a powerful personal CRM -- automatically. Here's everything that comes built in.

📅

Zero Data Entry

Your calendar does the work. Who I Met automatically extracts contacts from your calendar events -- names, emails, companies, and meeting history. No manual entry required.

📋

Smart Lists

Organize contacts your way with custom lists. Tag contacts, create lists for specific groups, and filter your network by any criteria.

✨

Contact Enrichment

Turn an email address into a full professional profile. Get LinkedIn URLs, job titles, company info, and profile photos automatically.

📧

Mail Merge

Send personalized emails at scale. Create templates with merge fields and send to your lists directly from Who I Met.

🔍

Relationship Insights

See your complete meeting history with any contact at a glance. Know when you last met, how often you meet, and never lose context.

⚡

Instant Search

Find anyone in your network instantly. Search by name, company, email, or any tag. Results appear as you type.

🔒

Privacy Focused

Who I Met only reads your calendar metadata (titles, attendees, times). We never read email content or calendar event descriptions.

📆

Multiple Calendars

Connect Google Calendar and Outlook. Sync multiple calendars to capture your complete professional network.

⏳

3-Year Lookback

Import up to 3 years of calendar history. Never miss a past connection -- your existing network is already built.
