The Problem

Sales reps know the drill: after every meeting, there is a stack of admin waiting. Update the CRM. Log the call notes. Tag the contact. Schedule the follow-up. It is tedious, time-consuming, and the first thing to slip when things get busy.

Contacts fall through the cracks when reps are juggling dozens of active deals. A prospect you met at a conference three weeks ago? Forgotten. That warm intro from a mutual connection? Never logged. The follow-up you promised to send after last Tuesday's call? It is still sitting in your mental to-do list.

The result: deals stall, relationships go cold, and revenue slips away — not because of a bad product, but because of broken process.

Traditional CRMs are built for managers who want dashboards, not for reps who want to close deals. They demand data entry instead of enabling action. Who I Met flips that model.

How Who I Met Solves It

Who I Met automatically builds your contact history from your Google Calendar, so you never have to manually update a CRM again. Here is how it works at every stage of the sales cycle.

Before the Call

Pre-Call Prep

See your full meeting history with any prospect before your next call. Know what was discussed, who else was in the room, and when you last connected. Walk into every conversation with context — not cold.

After the Meeting

Post-Meeting Follow-Up

After every meeting, your contacts are automatically updated. Create follow-up lists and send personalized emails while you are still top of mind. No more "I'll get to it later" that turns into never.

Pipeline Management

Deal Stage Lists

Create smart lists for each stage of your pipeline. Move contacts between lists as deals progress. Always know where every deal stands — without switching between five different tools.

Features for Sales
