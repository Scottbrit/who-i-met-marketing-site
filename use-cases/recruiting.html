The Problem

Recruiters meet hundreds of candidates across phone screens, interviews, and events. Tracking them across spreadsheets, ATS systems, and email threads is a nightmare. Great candidates slip through because follow-up doesn't happen fast enough.

By the time you circle back to that strong candidate from three weeks ago, they've already accepted another offer. The information was scattered across too many tools, and the moment passed.

How Who I Met Solves It

Candidate Tracking

Every interview, phone screen, and coffee chat is automatically logged. See your full history with any candidate without digging through email. Know exactly when you first met, how many times you've spoken, and what meetings you've had together.

Talent Pipeline Lists

Create lists for different roles, stages, or specialties. Keep warm candidates organized and ready for when the right role opens. Move people between lists as they progress through your pipeline.

Enrichment for Profiles

Automatically enrich candidate profiles with LinkedIn URLs, current job titles, and company info. Build richer candidate profiles without manual research. Spend your time building relationships, not copying and pasting from LinkedIn.
