The problem isn't motivation—it's friction. Manually logging every person you met, remembering context, and tracking who you've reached out to is exhausting. By the time you sit down to do it, the moment has passed.

How WhoIMetWith Solves Conference Networking

If you scheduled coffee chats, 1:1s, or group meetings during the conference through your calendar, WhoIMetWith automatically captures every attendee. No manual entry. No scanning business cards. Just connect your Google Calendar and your conference contacts appear.

Before the Conference

Schedule all your meetings through Google Calendar. Even quick 15-minute catch-ups in the hallway—if it's on your calendar with attendees, we'll track it.

During the Conference

Focus on being present in conversations. When you meet someone new, add a quick calendar event with their email. That's it—they're now in your system.

After the Conference

Open WhoIMetWith and see everyone you met, organized by when you connected. Filter by the conference dates, create a "Conference 2024" list, and start your follow-ups with full context.

Pro Tip: Use Meeting Titles as Context

When creating calendar events during a conference, add context to the title: "Coffee with Sarah - interested in API partnership" or "Met at keynote - hiring for eng." WhoIMetWith preserves this context so you remember why you connected.
